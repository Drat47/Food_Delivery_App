Partner Onboarding

I want to partner my restaurant with Swiggy v
Please send your details to partner@swiggy.com or visit our partner portal to sign up.
What are the mandatory documents needed to list my restaurant? v
You need FSSAI license, GST number, PAN card, and a cancelled cheque.
I want to opt-out from Google reserve v
You can disable this in your Restaurant Partner app settings under integrations.
After I submit all documents, how long will it take to go live? v
Typically it takes 48-72 hours for verification and menu digitization.
